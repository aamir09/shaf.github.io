• Example-3: Motion Reasoning

SHAF, can you describe what the person is doing in this motion?

The person is walking forward, then they turn around and walk back slowly, and finally, they squat down and get back up.

Why does the person turn around after running forward?

The person might turn around to return to where they started or to change direction quickly.

Why do you think the person is walking back slowly?

Walking back slowly might be to make sure they're careful or to focus on something ahead.

What might the person be doing when they bend down and get back up?

Bending down and getting back up could be part of a exercise routine or a way to move comfortably while performing a task.
